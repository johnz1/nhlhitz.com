Since 2009, I have run an organized NHL Hitz 20-02 league. We exclusively play the game with six players. We have in-person tournaments, stat tracking, a physical and web-based soundboard, and game hacks for true widescreen and a variety of custom graphics. Until 2021, we played in-person. Starting in 2022, we have played online with PCSX2 and Parsec on a cloud-hosted gaming server.

Our server is in New York. If you live anywhere near the northeast region of the US and you want to play HITZ online with us, tweet us @nhlhitz2002 and ask for an invite.
